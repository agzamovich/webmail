KCC's Student E-Mail Web Logon



Student Email through a CUNY-wide partnership with Microsoft, we are offering students a state-of-the-art e-mail and communication suite. With consistent 24-hour reliability, the suite allows you to perform all common tasks online, e.g., e-mail, shared calendars, chatting. To look up your KBCC email address, please click:

https://inside.kingsborough.edu/lookup

Your Email Username is your firstname.lastname + the last two digits of your Emplid + @students.kbcc.cuny.edu Example: jane.doe78@students.kbcc.cuny.edu
Your Email Password is, your Capitalized Firstname initial + lowercase lastname initial + the first six digits of your Emplid number. Example: Jane doe,12345678 password is Jd123456

OR

Your Email Username is your firstname.lastname + the day of your date of birth. Example: Jane doe, 04/07/91 would be jane.doe07@students.kbcc.cuny.edu. Your Email Password is your Capitalized First name's initial + lowercase last name's initial + the last 4 digits of your Social Security Number + the day of your date of birth. Example: Jane doe, 6789, 04/07/91 would be Jd678907
